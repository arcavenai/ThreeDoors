# Story 4.6: "Better Than Yesterday" Multi-Dimensional Tracking

## Status: in-progress

## Story

**As a** user,
**I want** to see progress across multiple dimensions,
**So that** I celebrate improvement beyond just task count.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** accumulated session history, **When** a new session starts, **Then** the greeting includes multi-dimensional comparison: tasks completed, doors opened, mood trend, avoidance reduction, and streaks.
2. **Given** session history, **When** the greeting is displayed, **Then** comparison is day-over-day and week-over-week.
3. **Given** any direction of change, **When** messaging is generated, **Then** it is encouraging regardless of direction (e.g., "3 tasks today vs 5 yesterday ŌĆö every door opened counts").
4. **Given** limited terminal space, **When** dimensions are displayed, **Then** they are compact (single line or expandable).
